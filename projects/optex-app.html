OPTEX Vision App

Background

The OPTEX Vision App is an innovative application designed to connect to the OPTEX surveillance system, offering users real-time video streaming, event recording, and live notifications. It allows residential and commercial users to intuitively and securely manage their video surveillance remotely

What our Japanese partner has done with me…

Japanese Partner

OPTEX (オプテックス株式会社) is a brand specializing in sensing technology, headquartered in Shiga Prefecture, Japan, and was established in 1979. It is known for providing high-performance sensing solutions, and its products are widely used in fields such as security, building automation, and traffic control

Involvement

  • User Research

    • I collaborated with the OPTEX Japanese partner to conduct in-depth interviews, gathering their insights on the Japanese and European markets as well as user needs

  • Design Planning

    • Responsible for process design and creating low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototypes, focusing on simplifying navigation logic to ensure users can quickly grasp the operations

Concept

The design concept focuses on combining convenience, connectivity, and interactivity to create a seamless user experience

Insight

When designing the OPTEX Vision App, we adhered to OPTEX's consistent brand style, using minimalist design philosophy as the core principle, presenting a perfect blend of brand image and user experience
