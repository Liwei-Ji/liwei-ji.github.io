The Journey to Rebuild My Portfolio

👋 I'm Liwei Ji For years, I searched for the best way to showcase my work but realized it was time for a change

I started with Behance simple, easy to use, and great for uploading designs. But as my needs grew, its lack of flexibility limited how I could present my work

Next, I turned to Notion. It's excellent for organizing ideas but lacks interactivity and a professional feel. That's when I realized I needed a personalized online presence

I wanted a platform to unify my content across LinkedIn, Medium, and beyond. These platforms work well individually but fail to present a complete professional image. A dedicated website would give visitors a deeper understanding of me

More importantly, my website should evolve with me not just as a static portfolio, but as a reflection of my growth. Every update marks another step forward

This rebuild isn't just technical it's an investment in self improvement, a journey toward excellence
